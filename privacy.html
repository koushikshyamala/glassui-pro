Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

At GlassUI Pro, we believe in keeping things transparent. When you use our CSS generator, all rendering happens locally in your browser. We do not collect your personal keystrokes or design choices. If you create an account, we collect your basic email address for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

Any information we collect is strictly used to:

  • Provide and maintain the GlassUI platform.
  • Notify you about changes to our features.
  • Provide customer support when requested.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal essential cookies to keep you logged in and save your theme preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies to follow you across the web. Your designs are your own.

4. Data Security

We implement modern security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at privacy@glassui.com.
